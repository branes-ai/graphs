Layer 7: external memory across SKUs

External-memory characteristics. Bandwidth and pJ/byte trade off across technology generations: DDR5 desktop pays the highest energy per byte; LPDDR5 mobile / on-package is the sweet spot for edge inference; HBM3 (not in this catalog yet) drops below 10 pJ/B at the cost of die area. Hailo SKUs load weights from host DRAM at initialization and serve steady-state inference from on-chip SRAM, so their host-side numbers describe the cold-start path.
